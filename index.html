About the movie

Loneliness, friendship, hope.

Bruno is accidentally left when the car door shuts. Convinced his humans still love him, he navigates a city that suddenly feels too loud. Every sound is sharper, every shadow unfamiliar, yet he keeps moving.

Pip—a small grey kitten—slides a crust of bread toward him: “You look like you need this more than I do.” From that kindness, the story of “Left Behind” blooms into a duet about resilience and shared instinct.

Characters

Bruno & Pip

Glassmorphic dossiers honoring the courage of two unlikely companions.

Bruno

Gentle, earnest, and guided by memory. He forgives even while he aches: “Humans get busy… they forget.”

  • • Remembers soft blankets and warm meals.
  • • Listens for the squeak of his favourite toy in every alley.
  • • Believes love is waiting just beyond the next street.

Pip

Street-smart, agile, and deeply loyal. Pip survives by reading crowds, sensing danger, and sharing hope.

  • • Pushes bread toward Bruno before taking a bite.
  • • Teaches him to slip between bicycles, avoid fast wheels.
  • • Knows the city’s rhythm and where softness hides.
