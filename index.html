About the movie

Loneliness, friendship, hope.

London, 1:30 PM. The Smith family is busy packing items in large boxes. Mr. Smith shouts that the movers' truck is almost here. Archie runs down the stairs calling for Bruno, but in the chaos of moving, the dog is forgotten.

As the family drives away to start a new life in Derby, Bruno comes running toward the driveway. He tries to hop in, but the door shuts. The car pulls away. Bruno barks, runs, but the car disappears into traffic. From a clean and loved dog, he is now alone, dirty, and dripping from the gutter he tumbles into.

The Journey

Bruno walks along pavements, evading moving feet. He's getting hungry and tired, but the busy streets are no place for a dog who once slept on soft blankets and ate warm meals. The world feels larger than ever before. Every noise seems sharper. Every stranger looks unfamiliar.

Hearts

At night, Bruno remembers the little girl who held his face in her hands, whispering secrets into his fur. He remembers the scent of his family's jackets. He remembers the squeak of his favourite toy.

Characters

Bruno & Pip

Glassmorphic dossiers honoring the courage of two unlikely companions.

Bruno

A loyal family dog, cherished for 3 years. Left behind in the rush of moving day. He chases the car until he tumbles into a gutter, panting and drooling. Reality hits: for the first time in his life, he is on his own, alone.

Pip

A small grey kitten with bright and mischievous eyes. She knows the streets better than any map could show. When she sees Bruno, she pads toward him, pushing a crust of bread with her head.

The Chase

Bruno gives a mighty chase before tumbling into a gutter, panting and drooling. From a clean and loved dog, he is now dirty and dripping. Reality hits: for the first time, he is on his own. "I don't think they meant to leave me. Humans get busy… they forget."

Scene 3

The Streets

Bruno walks along pavements, evading moving feet. He's getting hungry and tired. He finds a park and sleeps where there's less activity. The world feels larger than ever. Every noise seems sharper. Every stranger looks unfamiliar.

Scene 4

Pip's Kindness

A small grey kitten pads toward Bruno, pushing a crust of bread. "You look like you need this more than I do." Pip teaches Bruno to slip between crowds, avoid bicycles, and sense danger long before it arrives.

Scene 5

The Accident

Bruno and Pip run to cross the highway. A car hoots. "Bang"—Bruno is sent flying. Pip screams for help. A woman dials emergency responders. When Bruno wakes, he finds himself in a comfortable bed in the vet clinic.
